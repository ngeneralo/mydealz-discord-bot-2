Start the bot in channel:
**!start**

Stop the bot in channel:
**!stop**

Add new price rule:
**!add rule** <role id> <min price> <max price> <min discount> 

Add new buzzword:
**!add buzzword** <role id> <buzzwords>

Delete price rule:
**!del rule** <index>

Delete buzzword:
**!del buzzword** <index>

List all rules:
**!rules**

List all buzzwords:
**!buzzwords**

Help:
**!help**

Ratespiel:
**!guess**
